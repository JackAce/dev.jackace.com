Privacy Policy Overview

This privacy policy covers how JackAce.com treats personal information it receives from visitors. We understand that privacy is a huge concern for a large part of our audience, who often deal with cryptocurrencies and gambling sites.

How do you Guarantee Privacy?

As of today, JackAce doesn't require registration, so there is not a lot of information we'd be able to share with third parties.

What information is collected by JackAce.com?

We're not collecting any data at the moment, but we do use Google Analytics and Google Adsense, which both track user activity via cookies.

What about Cookies?

Google Analytics and Google Adsense make use of cookies to identify repeat visitors and to determine what ads to show.
