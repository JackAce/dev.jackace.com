--- layout: book-details book-id: playing-blackjack-as-a-business title: Playing Blackjack as a Business - JackAce Book Review ---

Lawrence Revere (a.k.a Spec Parsons) revolutionized Blackjack with his simplified "Plus-Minus" count, the predecessor to the widely used "Hi Low" count used today.

Revere also created the first color Blackjack Basic Strategy charts, whose color scheme is the basis for Basic Strategy charts printed today.

I LOVE this book and it was the first blackjack book I ever read, but it is very outdated and most paperback copies don't have the charts printed in color.
