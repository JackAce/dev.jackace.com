So, I'm continuing my efforts to help make tools available to people wishing to learn how to solve the Rubik's Cube.

I have one 60 second video for each of the 18 moves: U, U', D, D', L, L', R, R', F, F', B, B', E, E', M, M', S, and S',

All 18 videos are available in this YouTube Playlist.
