Die Hard Roulette players play single zero Roulette. And Die Hard Roulette players use the Racetrack layout.

The Racetrack layout is associated with traditional Roulette played in France (the word "Roulette" itself is French for "small wheel").

The Racetrack Roulette layout supplements the "Outside" bets on the American Roulette layout and provides additional options for multi-number betting that you can't easily do without making multiple bets.

The types of bets you can make on the Racetrack layout can be divided into two different types of wagers: X and Y.
