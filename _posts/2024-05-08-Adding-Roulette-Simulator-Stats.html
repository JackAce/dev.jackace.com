The onslaught of new features for the JackAce Roulette System Catalog continues! We're adding simulation results to the catalog!

These are the results of hitting various winning goals using the JackAce Roulette Simulator, which performs thousands of trials of the designated roulette system.

As many of you know, I have written a Roulette Simulator that is featured in many of my Roulette videos. I use the results of the simulations to help give my overall rating of each system. Now you can see these results in the system details as well!

I haven't recorded simulations for every system. In fact, I will probably only run sims for systems that either I or CEG give a good rating.

If you want me to add simulator stats for your favorite system, let me know! The best way to get a hold of me is probably through Discord.
