The system is named Secret Fast Food Menu for YouTube search engine optimization. It's meant to be a false positive search result when someone is looking for secret menu items.

Bet any 9 splits, but the system author suggest betting the splits that have like colors.

Ultimately, you can pick any 18 numbers, including any of the Even Money bets.

If you hit, you're supposed to double up the bets by just betting $1 on each of the numbers straight up. Then win or lose, you return to the first tier.

This system just seems like a two-step Piroli. You just try to win 2 in a row instead of 3 or 4. I don't think the wins are big enough and I don't like making 9 and 18 bets at a time. I'd rather just make an even money bet.
