This is a different system than the system reviewed on Jan 22, 2022 with the same name.

Bets:

  • Bet $15 on any of the even money bets.
  • Bet $15 one two Dozens.
  • Bet $20 two Columns.
  • Bet $5 on all the reds in a snake pattern, plus $5 on the {% include roulette-spot.html number="0" -%}-{%- include roulette-spot.html number="00" %} split.

This system seems like Hopscotch, but with the snake pattern on the 4th tier bet. Can't say I'm a fan of the snake bet.
