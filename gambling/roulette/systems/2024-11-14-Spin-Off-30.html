--- layout: roulette-system title: Spin Off 3.0 - Roulette System Review ---

Positive Progression (Spectrum) system. If you only win $25 on the first tier, go to tier 2. If you win $200, then go to tier 3. If you win on tier 2, also go to tier 3.

On tier 1, you can bet on any Dozen and any Column.

On tier 2, you can press either the Dozen or the Column.

On tier 3, bet $10 on any 10 Splits.
