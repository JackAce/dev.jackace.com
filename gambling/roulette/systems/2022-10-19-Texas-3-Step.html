The tagline for this video is "Turn $40 into $300", but that's not possible. The minimum you'd need for this betting sequence is $50.

Betting sequence for this system:

    Bet $10 on 4 Corner bets.
    If that hits, bet $50 each on 2 Dozens or Columns (not overlapping).
    If that hits, bet $10 each on 15 numbers Straight-up.
