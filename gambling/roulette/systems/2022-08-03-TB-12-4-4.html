First spin was a blank spin to establish which Dozen to avoid.

Bet $30 on a dozen, and cover the other dozen with 4 Split and 4 Straight-up bets.

You could also recreate this with 2 $10 corner bets and 2 $10 Split bets along with the $30 dozen.
