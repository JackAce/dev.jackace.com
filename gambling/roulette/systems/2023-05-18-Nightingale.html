--- layout: roulette-system title: Nightingale - Roulette System Review ---

This is a twist on the system Hopscotch.

This system doesn't really make sense.

Progression is $10, $20, $30 (?)... Alex was all over the map with the bets and I don't really understand why the bets seem to increase at different rates.

The point of Martingale is to prevent getting wiped out, not to springboard a win, so I don't see why this system would be attractive to anyone. You're increasing your starting bet and betting way more than you might be down, which adds a ton of risk to a system that is meant to be low risk.
