You're supposed to bet the 3 neighbors of the last 3 spins (21 numbers in total). If you double up your bets, that's fine.

This is a ridiculous system with absolutely no basis for making these bets. Betting the neighbors is a complete waste of time.

You're betting $5 on 21 numbers. If you only have $5 on the number, you'll win $75. If you double up a number, it'll win $255 if it hits. Tripled up numbers will win $435.

Alex was down $190 at the end, but he should have been down $195.
