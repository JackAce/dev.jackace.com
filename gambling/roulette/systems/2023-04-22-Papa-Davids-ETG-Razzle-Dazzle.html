This system didn't appear to have a Name, I gave it one!

  • Bet $1 on any Straight-up bet.
  • If that loses, bet $2 on a Split bet containing the previous number
  • If that loses, bet $3 on a Street bet containing the starting number
  • If that loses, bet $4 on a Corner bet containing the starting number
  • If that loses, bet $6 on a Double Street bet containing the starting number
  • If that loses, bet $12 on a Dozen bet containing the starting number
  • If that loses, bet $24 each on two Dozens containing the previous Dozen bet and any other Dozen bet
  • If that loses, bet $120 on an Even Money bet containing your starting number
