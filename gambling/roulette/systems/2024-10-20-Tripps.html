--- layout: roulette-system title: Tripps - Roulette System Review ---

This is a variant of Triple Entry Max Climax.

Start with two $10 bets on two Dozens. If that wins, then bet $10 on three even money bets (non-overlapping). This is tier 2.

If you lose, reset to step 1.

If you win 2/3 of the even money bets, then bet tier 3 - two $20 bets on 2 dozens.

If you win 3/3 of the even money bets, then bet tier 4 - $10 on Low, $10 on Red, $10 on the 1st and 3rd columns.
