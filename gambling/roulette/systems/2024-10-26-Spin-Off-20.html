This is a Positive Progression system that is a variant of the Spin Off system. If you hit on step 1, go to step 2. If you win $75 on step 2, go to step 3. You can stay on step 3 until you double your money. Reset to step 1 when you lose.

You can choose which dozens you want to bet on for the first 2 tiers. For tier 3, you can pick whatever 10 splits you want to bet so long as they don't overlap.
