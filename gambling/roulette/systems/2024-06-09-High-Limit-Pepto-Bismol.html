--- layout: roulette-system title: High Limit Pepto Bismol - Roulette System Review ---

This is a 4 leg D'Alembert system betting on 2 Dozens and 2 Columns. The 2 Dozens increase and decrease as a single leg. The 2 Columns increase and decrease as their own leg.

The Dozen bet should always be the 1st dozen. The Column bet follows the last spin.

$4000 buy-in and walk when you're up $1000.
