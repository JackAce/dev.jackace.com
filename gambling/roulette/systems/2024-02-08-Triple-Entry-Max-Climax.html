Step 1: Bet $25 each on 3 Even Money bets. If two bets hit, go to step 2 ($100). If all 3 bets hit, go to step 3 ($150).

If you only win 1, then bet $50 on a single dozen.

If you lose all 3, remain at step 1.

Step 2: Bet $50 each on two Dozens or Columns.

Step 3: Bet $15 each on 10 numbers straight up. Go to back to Step 1 win or lose.

Step 4: When you lose step 1, bet $50 on a single dozen.

Step 5: When you lose step 4, bet $15 on the 0/00 Split.

I am not as happy with this strategy as CEG. It's a good positive progression system, but I don't think you have suffiecient opportunity to win a large jackpot. The betting level is too high and the jackpot is too low.
