Another GARBAGE system where you're betting on 36 or more numbers at a time.

You should never bet on more than 35 numbers at a time, because you end up in a Bet Nullification situation. Watch this video to learn more about it:

Alex was up $115 at the end, but he should have been up $140. In fact, there's no possible way he could have been up $115 given this betting pattern. Every win and loss ends with a 0, so his overall win should also end with a zero.

About half-way through the video, Dennis suggested they change the streets that they bet in the 2nd dozen to the 13, 16, and 19 streets.

This is illustrated in the 4th tier for this system.

This is a MUCH better way to play because it eliminates the bet nullification situation, although it opens up more WHACKS (which is fine).
