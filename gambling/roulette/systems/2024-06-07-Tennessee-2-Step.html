--- layout: roulette-system title: Tennessee 2 Step - Roulette System Review ---

Bet tier 1. When that wins, go to tier 2. When tier 2 hits TWICE, go to tier 3.

Any loss, reset to tier 1.

Walk when you're up $500 or more.

This is a rough system. I really don't like adding a positive progression on top of a system with such high coverage. I think it's just a recipe for disaster. You're hedging too much and winning too little. All the pushes are going to cost you because you're depending on too few numbers to hit to proceed.
