Betting Strategy

  • Bet $25 on any Even Money bet. If this loses, start over.
  • When the Even Money bet wins, bet $25 on two non-overlapping Dozen or Column bets. If this loses, start over.
  • Bet Tier 3 on any of the 3 dozens.
