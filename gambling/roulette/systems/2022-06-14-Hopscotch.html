Betting system is the following:

  • $5 on any even money bet
  • If you win, $5 each on two non-overlapping Dozen/Column Bets
  • If you win, $10 on any even money bet
  • If you win, $10 each on two non-overlapping Dozen/Column Bets
  • If you lose go back to the $5 even money bet.
