--- layout: roulette-system title: Good Things Come in 3s - Roulette System Review ---

This starts out with two Dozen bets, then launches into Triple Entry Max Climax.

When you bet the 3 even money bets, if you win 1/3, then you drop down to two Dozen bets with the remaining money. If you win 2/3, press the winnings into two dozen bets. When you win 3/3, then start over at tier 1.

I think this system is just too complicated with too many possibilites for each step and there's little opportunity to win big.
