Bet $300 across 5 Double Streets ($60 each). When that wins, play the $10/$10/$15/$10/$15 Double Street bets with the $60 win (a.k.a. the "casino's money").

Reset to step 1 if you lose.

I don't really like or dislike this system. I think it's pretty hard to recover once you get whacked, though.
