Not to be confused with "Suck MY Luck".

Start off with a blank spin or just look at the last number (color) that hit. You only care about the column.

Bet $30 on that color, bet $60 each on the 2nd and 3rd columns and bet $5 on every matching color in the 1st columns (6x$5 bets). That's a total of $180 bet.

Not really a fan of systems like this where you bet on a ton of numbers, especially red/black and not a fan of switching bets so often. It's just a lot of moving parts trying to catch streaks which doesn't really affect your overall odds.
