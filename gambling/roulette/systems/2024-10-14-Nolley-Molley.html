--- layout: roulette-system title: Nolley Molley - Roulette System Review ---

Ladder system where you press $10 when you lose and you press the single Dozen/Column bet when you win.

When you bet two dozens and one hits, you make the same bet, but press the win on the winning bet (making the bet lobsided). When you make a lobsided bet and the big bet wins, you even the bets out for the next spin. If you win $60 on a lobsided bet, then reset to step 1.
