If one of those hits, you take the winnings and divide it in half and bet two dozens.

If you win the 2 dozens bet, go back to step 1 ($10).

If you lose the 2 dozens bet, continue with the NEXT step from where you were... (as if you didn't bet the 2 dozens bet)
