--- layout: roulette-system title: EDM - Roulette System Review ---

EDM stands for "Each Dozen Middle". This is a Flat Bet system that is supposed to be a good "comp system".

Bet 3 Double Streets: $30, $30, and $50. And bet 7 Split bets. You can move these all around however you want, just make sure there are no overlaps.

There are 6 wins of {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=120 %} and 6 losses of {% include win-loss-amount.html amount=-180 %}. The rest are just pushes.

I'm not really a fan of "comp systems".
