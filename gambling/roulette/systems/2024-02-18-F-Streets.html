--- layout: roulette-system title: F-Streets - Roulette System Review ---

Start with a blank ($0 bet) spin.

Bet $10 on the street that hits (wait for the next spin if 0/00 hits).

If your street bet his, then start over (blank spin step).

Keep betting the streets that hit until you have 4 street bets out.

If you miss with 4 street bets out, then Fibonacci each street bet (go to $20/street immediately)
