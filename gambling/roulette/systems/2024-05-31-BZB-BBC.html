--- layout: roulette-system title: BZB BBC - Roulette System Review ---

BBC stands for "Big/Black/Columns". This is a Positive Progression system.

Bet $50 on the Big (High) numbers.

When that wins, add a $50 bet to Black.

When both bets win, add two $50 Column bets. On a push in step 2, stay on step 2.

Any loss resets to step 1.
