--- layout: roulette-system title: The Balls Cover All - Roulette System Review ---

System progression:

  • Bet 2 Dozens at $15 each
  • If that hits, then bet 2 Dozens at $20 each and $10 on an uncovered Double Street.
  • If that hits, then add $5 to each of the 3 bets.

I only included images for the first 3 tiers of this system.

I don't like that the bets don't increase in the same proportion. I think it would be better to increase the Double Street by $10 instead of $5 to keep the payouts identical.

It seems like you have to have a VERY long streak of wins if you want to walk away with a profit. Most of the time you're going to hover around being even.
