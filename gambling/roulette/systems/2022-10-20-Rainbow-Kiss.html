I will usually downgrade a system if it is needlessly complicated and can be made a lot simpler without changing any of the statistical characteristics.

This betting pattern involves 15 bets, but can be replicated with only 3.

You can achieve a Congruent system by just betting $50 on the 1st Dozen, $60 on the 1st Column, and $25 on an oncovered corner.
