Positive Progression system - Bet $25 on a Dozen. If that wins, bet $25 each on 2 Dozens. If that wins, bet $25 on a single column and $25 each on two corner bets that overlap on that column. If that bet wins up the middle, bet $25 on one column, one dozen and $10 on a number in the middle column straight-up and $10 on each of the 4 splits containing that same number.
