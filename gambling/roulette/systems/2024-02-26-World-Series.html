You start with 7 x $75 bets. You're going for a "best of 7" series...

Bet $50 on an even money bet. If that wins...

Bet $50 on a Dozen or Column. If that wins...

Bet $150 on an Even money bet. If that wins...YOU WIN A GAME and go onto the next "game" until you win/lose 4 out of 7.

If you lose any of the above bets, then you have $25 left in your game. Bet $25 on a Dozen or Column. If that wins, then go to Step 1 above.
