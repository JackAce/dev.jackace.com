--- layout: roulette-system title: Ploppy 3-2 DD - Roulette System Review ---

Start with a $30 bet on an even money bet, $20 on a column. Increase your bet when you lose using a $15 unit size on the even money bet, and $10 unit size on the column bet. Use D'Alembert to move up or down with the LARGE win (win both bets) as the factor for moving back down.

This is a dumb system. Way too complicated and there's no real way to get out of the hole you're going to dig when you lose.
