--- layout: roulette-system title: Ride or Die 2.0 - Roulette System Review ---

This is the scaled up version of Ride or Die, with $50 units instead of $5 units. It also only has 20 bullets instead of 50.

For tier 4, you're supposed to bet $50 on 7 numbers, straight up. That will get around the max inside payout for many lower limit Roulette tables.

Unfortunately, Alex and David never got past tier 2 of this 4 tiered system.
