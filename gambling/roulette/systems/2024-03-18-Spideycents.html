Start with Double Street Martingale. If you win $100, play with the winnings until you lose it. Got back to step 1.

Second step is two $25 dozen bets.

If that wins, 3rd step is to bet $50 on an even money bet.

If that wins, 4th step is $80 (should it be $75???) on a bunch of numbers.

After the 4th step, always reset to step 1.

I would improve this system by betting more on fewer numbers on the 4th tier.
