Positive Progression

Tier 1: $25 on 5 Double Streets.

If that wins, Tier 2: $25 on a Dozen or Column

If that wins, Tier 3: $25 on 2 Dozens

If that wins, Tier 4: $25 on 3 Corners (or $75 on a Dozen)

If that wins, go to Tier 2

If you ever lose, go to Tier 1
