The Total Amount Bet section displays your total amount bet (duh).

The EV/Spin is your expected loss for the given bets.

The Comp Value/Spin is your approximate comp value per spin (20% back from your expected loss).

The Win/Loss per Spot section shows how much you will win or lose for each result.

The Equity per Spot section shows how much you can bet on each number Straight-up instead of betting on any of the multi-number bets.

The Outcome Graph shows you a graph of every outcome, from worst result to best result over all possible results. After exhausting all possible results, you should be down 2 full bets in double zero, and 1 full bet in single zero.

The Outcome Graph can be used to identify congruent and similar betting patterns.
